# Tradeoff Matrix

## Product Decisions Behind Continue Watching

| Tradeoff | Benefit | Cost |
|-----------|-----------|-----------|
| Convenience vs Discovery | Faster resume | Reduced content exploration |
| Simplicity vs Control | Low user effort | Limited customization |
| Retention vs Homepage Real Estate | High feature visibility | Less recommendation space |

---

## Analysis

### Convenience vs Discovery

Netflix prioritizes helping users continue existing viewing behavior.

This improves engagement but may reduce exposure to new content.

---

### Simplicity vs User Control

Automatic tracking requires almost no effort.

However, users cannot easily customize the section.

---

### Retention vs Homepage Real Estate

The row occupies highly valuable homepage space.

Netflix accepts this because continued consumption strongly influences retention.

---

## PM Insight

Every retention-focused feature creates opportunity costs elsewhere in the user journey.

The goal is not to eliminate tradeoffs but to choose the right ones.
